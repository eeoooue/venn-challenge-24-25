The Venn Challenge 24'/25'

An AI-collaborative programming competition featuring eight custom problems themed around cryptography, media, and word games.

Over the course of two and a half hours, showcase your skills in working with generative AI technologies to take on new challenges.

Visit the repository to view the questions, or download them as a package from the releases page.

To check your answers, generate a hash with the hex grid and compare it against the validation patterns.
